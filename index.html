About Me

Welcome to my world of botanical art. I'm Jeanine Gribbin, an artist passionate about capturing the intricate beauty of nature through my paintings. Inspired by the Art Nouveau movement, I strive to create pieces that blend the organic forms of plants with the flowing, decorative style of this timeless aesthetic.
